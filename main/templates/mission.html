Our Mission
Our mission is your success

Our mission is to provide rapid publication of high quality original works within one month of final acceptance. Manuscripts will be accepted with the understanding that they report original unpublished works and are not under consideration for publication by any other journal. All manuscripts submitted to WJBB will be subject to review by three reviewers. High confidence is maintained to all the submitted Manuscripts, having access only by the Editor-in-Chief, the Managing Editor, the journal's secretary and the reviewers.
