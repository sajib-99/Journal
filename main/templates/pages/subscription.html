Journal Subscription
General
  • Subscription will be activated only after the receipt of the full payment.
  • Subscription rates will be revised every year.
  • World Journal of Biotechnology and Bioengineering authority reserves all the right to change and amend the subscription policy.
  • Institutions & companies should place an order on the letterhead on their respective institute or company.
  • World Journal of Biotechnology and Bioengineering can merge the issues if required and can provide the subscriber the merged issue.
Payment
  • Payments can be made through Master Card, Visa Card, American Express, PayPal, etc globally.
  • Once the order is processed, the subscription fee will not be refunded unless World Journal of Biotechnology and Bioengineering ceases its publication.
  • The Publisher can change subscription charges & conditions and at the time of receipt of order only, recent rates will be applicable.
